About Me

Timeline 2020s Menswear

Yuhang Hu

My name is Yuhang Hu. I earned my undergraduate degree in Fashion Design, where I explored garment construction, silhouette development, and creative expression through material experimentation. Studying fashion shaped how I observe culture, identity, and the evolution of style. My interests extend beyond clothing into how visual storytelling, history, and technology shape the future of menswear and digital fashion experiences. I continue researching how design can bridge personal expression, cultural influence, and modern aesthetics.
